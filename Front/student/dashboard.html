Tips for Providing Effective Feedback
  • Be specific about what worked well or needs improvement
  • Focus on the course content and teaching methods, not personal matters
  • Provide constructive suggestions for improvement
  • Avoid personal attacks or disrespectful language
  • Don't focus solely on negative aspects
  • Avoid vague comments like "good course" or "bad instructor"
